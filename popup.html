YouTube Video Effects™

« Tips »

» If you enable AE, your audio will be shared to extension to apply filters to improve sound quality. You will see this status on Tab. If you don't want to share sound with extension, you just disable it.

» In this sharing mode, the fullscreen mode of YouTube will work unperfectly (from Chrome 55 and above), so you need to press F11 key to switch to the fullscreen mode. The fullscreen mode will work back normally if you disable AE. This is a Chromium bug and we already reported to Chromium team. They will fix this issue soon.

» The adblock feature is now removed from this extension, because Google did not allow us to do this.

» The Picture in Picture feature is activated on Chrome 69+. To use PinP, on YouTube page, when you are watching video, you just press command F9.
